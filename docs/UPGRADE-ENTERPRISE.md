# Upgrade To Enterprise

Community is free and full-featured, but it does not include support, SLA,
onboarding, hotfix priority, resale rights, or white-label rights.

Upgrade to Enterprise when you need:

- cabinet, agency, ESN, or client delivery rights;
- managed-service or white-label resale;
- support contract, SLA, onboarding, and release assistance;
- priority fixes or guided migrations;
- commercial terms for client portfolios.

Enterprise uses the private distribution repository, signed license material,
and Enterprise release manifests.
